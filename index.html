Hi, I’m Adrien, a senior product designer based in Paris, working across product strategy, interface systems, and fast prototyping.

Right now, I’m building , a planning tool for product teams that connects vision, user research, and everyday decisions.

Before that, I worked with startups and internal teams on SaaS, mobile, and AI products. I like problems where the interface has to make something complex feel clear without making it stupid. I’ve worked on redesigns, onboarding flows, dashboards, design systems, and prototypes realistic enough to end vague meetings.

I’m useful when a product has too many possibilities and not enough shape. I structure, test, and push the details until the usage feels obvious. Polished demos matter less to me than interfaces that hold up under real pressure.

I’m interested in applied AI, creative tools, maps, personal memory, and products that give people more control. On Saturdays, I sketch small apps, take long walks, and write too many ideas in files I pretend are organized.
