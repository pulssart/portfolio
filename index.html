Hi, I’m Adrien. Product designer in Paris.

I’m a senior product designer currently working at , where I design compliance tools powered by AI. Most of my work sits between complex systems, human behavior, and interfaces that need to stay understandable even when the underlying logic becomes messy as hell.

Before that, I spent several years at working across products like Lemlist, Lemcal, and Lemwarm, helping scale the product design side from startup chaos to a much more mature SaaS ecosystem. Earlier in my career, I also worked at , which probably explains my obsession with details, clarity, and interactions that feel obvious without needing a tutorial.

I like products where the challenge is not just making things beautiful, but making complexity feel calm. I’ve worked on CRMs, AI workflows, dashboards, onboarding systems, design systems, internal tools, sales platforms, and increasingly, agentic AI interfaces. My favorite projects are usually the ones where nobody really knows what the final shape should be yet.

I’m useful when a product has too many directions, too many features, or too much abstraction. I tend to bring structure, simplify the mental model, prototype fast, and push until the interaction feels natural enough that people stop thinking about the interface itself.

Outside of work, I build a lot of side projects around AI, creative tooling, memory, storytelling, realtime interfaces, and generative visuals. Recently I’ve been experimenting with apps like Echo, Flux, Natura, and StoryTime AI. Most start as “just a small experiment” and then suddenly it’s 2am and I’m redesigning an onboarding flow for a fake feature nobody asked for. Which is honestly still one of my favorite things in the world.
